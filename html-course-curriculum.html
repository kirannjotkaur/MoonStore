Course Overview
About This Curriculum

This curriculum is designed for complete beginners through intermediate learners. Each module builds on the last, combining theory with hands-on practice. By the end, you will be able to build professional, accessible, and standards-compliant websites.

💡
How to use this curriculumEach module includes theory, exercises, and a mini-project. Complete them in order. Estimated time per day: 1–2 hours for 4–6 weeks to complete the full course.
