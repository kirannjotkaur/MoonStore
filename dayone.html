Day 1 — Sell Study Notes

Turn your class notes into a digital product.

Opportunity

Thousands of students search for simple revision notes before exams.

Platform

Share previews on Instagram, Telegram or student groups.

1. Choose a Subject

Select a subject you understand well and start making helpful notes.

2. Create Clean Notes

3. Convert to PDF

4. Sell Online

Post previews on Instagram and sell the complete notes.
