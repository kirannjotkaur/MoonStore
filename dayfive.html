What Is Faceless YouTube?

This is a YouTube business where you create videos without showing your face. You can use AI tools, stock footage and voiceovers to upload content.

No Face Needed

You don’t need to show your identity.

AI Powered

Create scripts, voice and videos using AI tools.

Passive Income

Videos can generate income over time.

How It Works

1️⃣ Choose a niche (Facts, Motivation, AI, Luxury)
2️⃣ Create script using AI
3️⃣ Generate voiceover
4️⃣ Add stock videos or clips
5️⃣ Upload on YouTube
