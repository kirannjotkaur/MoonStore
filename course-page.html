Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do I need business experience to take this course? +

No. Each idea is explained from first principles. Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur or a manager looking to improve your own team's efficiency, the content is designed to be immediately applicable.

How much time do I need each day? +

Plan for 45–60 minutes per day to complete all five sessions. The field challenge at the end of each day can be done in an additional 20–30 minutes, or spread across the week.

Are these real businesses I can actually start? +

Yes — every idea in this course has real-world examples. Each one is structured as a viable business model, not just a concept. Day 7 shows you how to combine them all into a single offering.

Can I use these ideas to improve my current employer's operations? +

Absolutely. The frameworks, templates, and session content are equally useful whether you're starting a new venture or bringing efficiency improvements to your current role.

Is there a community or cohort? +

The course is self-paced, but we host a monthly live Q&A for anyone progressing through the curriculum. Details are shared on Day 1.
