Turn Your Study Notes Into Income

Most students hate making notes. If you are someone who can create clear notes, you can turn them into a simple digital product and start earning.

Why This Idea Works

Students always search for short revision notes before exams. Instead of spending hours making notes themselves, they prefer using ready-made summaries. If you create clear and structured notes, they can become valuable study material.

How To Start

Step 1

Choose a subject you understand well such as Political Science, Sociology, English or Mathematics.

Step 2

Create clean and organized notes using tools like Google Docs, Canva or AI writing tools.

Step 3

Convert your notes into PDF format so they can be easily shared and downloaded.

Step 4

Sell your notes through Instagram, Telegram groups, WhatsApp groups or your own website.

Example Income

If 20 students buy your notes for ₹49
