Day 2 Business Idea

Dropshipping

Sell products online without storing inventory.

Understand The Model

How Dropshipping Works

Customer
→
Your Online Store
→
Supplier
→
Product Delivered

Customer orders from your store → you forward the order → supplier ships the product.

Step By Step Process

1. Find A Product

Look for trending products people want to buy.

2. Create Store Page

Create a simple product page or mini store.

3. Promote On Social Media

Use Instagram reels or posts to attract buyers.

4. Supplier Ships Product

The supplier sends the product to the customer.

Product Ideas

Phone Accessories
LED Lights
Laptop Stands
Fitness Products
Study Lamps
Wireless Earbuds

Profit Example

₹200 – ₹500 per sale

Example:
Sell product for ₹999
Supplier price ₹699
Profit = ₹300

Why Students Like Dropshipping

No Inventory

You don't need to store products.

Low Risk

You pay the supplier only after you get an order.

Flexible

You can run the business from your phone.
